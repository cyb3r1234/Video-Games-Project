Fruit-Ninja

Fruit Ninja is a popular mobile game where you play as a ninja slicing fruit that is thrown into the air. You swipe your finger across the screen to mimic the slicing motion, aiming to cut the fruit in half. The more fruit you slice, the higher your score.
